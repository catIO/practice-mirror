About Practice Mirror

Part of the Practice Mate Suite of Tools for Musicians

Practice Mirror is a web-based tool designed to help musicians record practice sessions for instant self-evaluations or for sharing with a teacher via direct YouTube uploads.

By acting as a virtual, latency-free mirror, it lets you monitor instrument positioning, posture, and play mechanics in real-time, record performance clips locally, and securely upload them directly to your YouTube channel to share with instructors.

Latency-Free Recording

Capture your practice sessions with high-performance audio and video feeds directly in your browser. Recordings are generated entirely locally on your device for instant self-evaluation.

YouTube Integration

Integrate with your YouTube channel through secure Google Identity Services to upload recordings seamlessly. Great for sharing lessons with teachers or maintaining a public practice log.

Privacy by Design

We never run analytics, track your sessions, or upload your videos to our servers. Your camera stream, microphone, and video files stay on your device at all times.

Offline-First Utility

Designed as a Progressive Web App (PWA), Practice Mirror works flawlessly offline once loaded, ensuring you can practice even in rooms with poor internet reception.

The Practice Mate Ecosystem

Practice Mirror is a member of the Practice Mate project—a collection of open, privacy-focused, zero-tracker utilities built to empower musicians' practice routines.
