Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Practice Mirror handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience.

Google API Services & User Data

Practice Mirror integrates with Google API Services to support video uploads. We strictly adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Practice Mirror (YouTube Integration)

Data Access & Collection: Practice Mirror requests OAuth permission to access your YouTube account to perform video uploads (youtube.upload scope). We do not collect, read, or process any other personal information or channel files.

Data Usage: We use your Google account access and YouTube credentials solely to upload the practice recording video file directly to your channel as requested. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other business purpose.

Data Sharing & Transfers: We do not share, transfer, or disclose any Google user data or YouTube upload information to third parties. All authentication details and uploads are processed directly client-side in your browser.

Data Retention & Deletion: We do not store or retain any Google user data or recording files on any external servers. The video file is held temporarily in the browser's local memory during the active upload session and is immediately deleted when the upload completes or the browser tab is closed.

Security & Protection: Security procedures are in place to protect the confidentiality of your data. All communication between the application, your browser, and Google APIs is fully encrypted in transit using industry-standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL/HTTPS) encryption.

General Data Handling

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account with us to use our tools.
  • Local Storage: Any application configurations, preferences, or recording parameters are stored locally in your browser (via LocalStorage or IndexedDB) and remain entirely on your device.
  • No Analytics/Tracking: We do not track your usage habits or sell data to advertising networks.

Third-Party Terms & Revocation

By using Practice Mirror, you also agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google's main Privacy Policy.

You can revoke access to your Google account at any time. To manage your app permissions, visit the Google Security Settings Page.

Questions & Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
